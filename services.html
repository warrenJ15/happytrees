Planting New Trees

We have planted over 100 trees in three different sites of Kali Bekasi! Most of our trees at the moment are pohon jati or teek trees in English.

Eco-friendly

Our project is completely eco-friendly, instead of using large amounts of polybags and one time use plastic bags, we rely on taurpalin bags for our plants.

Non-profited

Happy Trees is a non-profit project where all of our proceeds are towards local communities in Bekasi as well as to various climate charities.
