About Happy Trees

Happy Trees Goal and Mission

On January 1st, 2020 we witnessed a catastrophe that we never saw coming our way. The water level rose higher than expected, eroding the embankment away and flooding the whole complex. It affected many parts of Indonesia, and Bekasi was one of the heavily-impacted zones. Floods are not a rare occurrence anymore, and it remains one of the largest issues in Indonesia. In order to counteract this, we plan to establish a project called Happy Trees that aims to lessen the impact of flooding in the city of Bekasi through the strategic planting of trees. By planting trees, we are helping the issue of flooding in the long-run.As Happy Trees, our mission is to help mitigate flooding in Kali Bekasi for decades. We saw first hand the catastrophic effects that flooding can have on us and we decided to prevent them from happening again by planting trees in the surrounding area. If the project turns out to be a success, we will start planting a larger variety of trees. Other than direct help towards the issue of flooding, we will also be raising awareness towards climate change and the importance of environmental protection through our social media platforms
